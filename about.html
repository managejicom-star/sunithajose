About

I'm Sunitha

A Happiness & Inner Work Coach creating frameworks, tools and experiences for inner transformation and conscious living within education and beyond.

My journey into this work began with a deep curiosity about people, emotions, and what it truly means to live well. Today, I create spaces for reflection, clarity, and conscious transformation.

In a world that often moves fast and outward, this is an invitation to turn inward— towards awareness, understanding, and intentional living. Through workshops, coaching journeys, and reflective tools, I work with individuals, students, and educators to help them reconnect with themselves and live more meaningfully.


The Approach
