Hello Feelings


 

A Social Emotional Learning Series for Ages 3–6

Helping Young Children Understand, Express and Grow Through Their Emotions.

Hello Feelings is a thoughtfully designed series of 7 emotional learning experiences for children aged 3 to 6, supporting them in recognising, expressing, and navigating their inner world through visuals, guided prompts, and simple reflective activities.

In a world where children are often taught what to think, this series gently teaches them how to feel, understand, and express.

Why Hello Feelings Matters?

Emotional awareness is not instinctive-it is learned.

When children are given the language and space to understand their feelings, they grow into individuals who are:

  • More confident in expressing themselves
  • Better equipped to handle challenges
  • More empathetic in their relationships
  • More connected to themselves

Hello Feelings builds this foundation early through creative, engaging, and meaningful learning experiences.

What Makes This Series Different?

This is not just a set of books.
It is a guided emotional learning system.
The series of 7 is designed to:

  • Introduce a range of core emotions in a simple, relatable way
  • Build emotional understanding progressively
  • Encourage reflection through gentle prompts
  • Create opportunities for meaningful conversations with adults
  • Support expression through creative, age-appropriate activities

The approach is playful yet intentional, structured yet deeply human.

Who It Is For?

  • Children aged 3–6
  • Parents who want to build emotional awareness at home
  • Encourage reflection through gentle prompts
  • Early years educators and schools
  • Facilitators working in early childhood well-being

What Children Experience?

Through Hello Feelings, children begin to:

  • Recognise and name their emotions
  • Express themselves with confidence
  • Understand that all feelings are valid
  • Build early emotional vocabulary and awareness


A Foundation for Lifelong Well-being

When children learn to understand their emotions early, they don’t just cope better—they grow better.
Hello Feelings is designed to be that starting point.
